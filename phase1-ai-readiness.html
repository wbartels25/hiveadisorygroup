The Starting Point

Why readiness determines everything.

Most AI initiatives fail not because of technology — but because organizations move before they know where they stand. They invest in tools their data cannot support, build teams before infrastructure is ready, or deploy AI into processes that have not been mapped.

The Hive AI Readiness Assessment is a structured diagnostic that prevents exactly that. In six focused weeks, our senior advisors work alongside your leadership team to build a complete, honest picture of your AI maturity and identify the highest-leverage moves to make first.

This is not a survey. It is a deep, collaborative engagement that produces insights your competitors do not have about themselves.

"Organizations that conduct formal AI readiness assessments are 2.4x more likely to achieve meaningful ROI from their first AI implementation."
