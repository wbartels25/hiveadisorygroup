About Hive Advisory Group

Hive Advisory Group is a vendor-neutral AI advisory firm built specifically for mid-market professional services firms — accounting, legal, consulting, and similar knowledge-based businesses up to $100M in revenue. We help leaders move past the hype and identify where AI will actually create value in their specific operating model, without being sold a tool.

Every engagement is senior-led. There is no junior handoff. The people you meet in the briefing are the people who do the work — and they bring decades of experience across enterprise transformation, data strategy, and professional services operations into every assessment and roadmap we build.

Our approach is structured, diagnostic-first, and tied to revenue and margin outcomes rather than feature checklists. We sit alongside your team through readiness, strategy, implementation, and adoption — so that AI becomes a durable capability inside your firm, not a stranded pilot.
